Frequently Asked Questions

How does Crazyrouter offer lower prices than direct API providers?
Crazyrouter aggregates demand across thousands of developers and negotiates volume pricing with providers. We pass the savings to you — no markup on most models.
Is the pricing calculator accurate?
The calculator uses current list prices from each provider and Crazyrouter's published rates. Actual costs may vary based on caching, batching, and prompt efficiency. Check crazyrouter.com/pricing for live rates.
What does "tokens" mean?
Tokens are the basic units AI models use to process text. Roughly 1 token ≈ 4 characters in English, or about ¾ of a word. A 1,000-word article is approximately 1,300 tokens.
Can I switch between models without changing my code?
Yes! Crazyrouter uses an OpenAI-compatible API. Just change the model name in your request — no SDK changes, no new API keys. Works with Python, Node.js, curl, LangChain, and more.
Do you support image and video generation models?
Yes. Crazyrouter supports DALL-E 3, Midjourney, Flux, Stable Diffusion, Sora 2, Kling, Runway, and more — all through a unified API.
