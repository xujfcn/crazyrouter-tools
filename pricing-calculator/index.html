Frequently Asked Questions

How does Crazyrouter offer lower prices?
Crazyrouter aggregates demand across thousands of developers and negotiates volume pricing with providers. Savings are passed directly to you — typically around 45% off official rates.
Are the prices in this calculator accurate?
The calculator uses official list prices from each provider and estimates Crazyrouter pricing at 55% of those rates. Actual costs may vary — check crazyrouter.com/models for real-time pricing.
What are tokens?
Tokens are the basic units AI models use to process text. Roughly 1 token ≈ 4 characters in English, or about ¾ of a word. A 1,000-word article is approximately 1,300 tokens.
Can I switch models without changing code?
Yes. Crazyrouter uses an OpenAI-compatible API. Just change the model name in your request — no SDK changes, no new API keys. Works with Python, Node.js, curl, LangChain, and more.
Do you support image and video generation?
Yes. Crazyrouter supports DALL-E 3, Midjourney, Flux, Stable Diffusion, Sora, Kling, Runway, and more — all through a unified API.
How is Crazyrouter different from OpenRouter?
Crazyrouter offers more models (627+ vs ~300), lower pricing, and supports image/video/audio generation models that OpenRouter doesn't. Same OpenAI-compatible API format.
